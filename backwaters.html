BACKWATERS

Life slows down as you travel through these meandering canals that weave their way through palm fringed tropical scenery. The gurgling waters and birdcalls that keep you company will fill your heart with a warmth like no other.


LET'S EXPLORE BACKWATERS IN KERALA

Alappuzha Backwaters

Alleppey, often referred to as the "Venice of the East," is the most popular backwater destination in Kerala. It is famous for its houseboat cruises, where tourists can drift along the narrow canals, passing through lush paddy fields, coconut groves, and rustic villages. The backwaters of Alleppey also lead to the serene Vembanad Lake, perfect for a calm retreat.


Ashtamudi Backwaters

A cruise trip through Ashtamudi Lake by seeing both banks of the lake and its backwater canals, coconut groves, and palm trees interwoven with towns and villages is a lifetime experience. Coconut husk retting for coir production, Fishing, and inland navigation services, the lake is the source of livelihood for many people. To ride boats, and explore village life, natural habitat, and abundant flora at its true essence, come to Ashtamudi.


Kumarakom Backwaters

Vembanad Lake, where a network of canals and rivers flows is an enchanting picnic spot and a fast developing backwater tourism destination. This lake, located 16 km from Kottayam at Kumarakom offers boating, fishing, and sightseeing experiences that are truly exhilarating.


Kochi Backwaters

Cochin backwaters are among the most famous backwaters of Kerala. Besides Kochi's parks, monuments of Fort Kochi and Mattancherry, the bustling markets, and malls, It has numerous backwaters, and hidden natural treasures kept unspoiled. A Cruise ride through the backwaters of Cochin by seeing the Chinese fishing nets and the fishermen on duty brings you a great sight of the historical land, with many islands scattered around.


Kozhikode Backwaters

The Elathur canal, Canolly Canal, and Kallai river make the Kozhikode backwaters. The food, its hospitality, and the Backwaters of Calicut is an evergreen combination that attracts people to this District. The backwaters of Kozhikode are the perfect escape to enjoy quality time with friends, family, and yourself.


Kasargod Backwaters

Kasargod backwaters are different from Alleppey backwaters because of their calmness and uncrowded destinations. Kasargod is the best option if you are a peace seeker and thinking of a trip away from the touristy hustle-bustle. Valiyaparamba and Chandragiri are two famous Kasargod backwater destinations that you can go to. A Cruise trip through these backwaters offers you an opportunity to explore the rich heritage of this district with seven languages and cultures. Kasargod is a magical land of green stretches, serene waterways, coconut grooves, local villages, authentic food, and unspoiled islands.
